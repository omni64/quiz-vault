# Landlocked countries

- **~44 landlocked countries** in the world (no sea coast).
- **Two "doubly landlocked" states** (landlocked *and* surrounded only by landlocked countries) — a top chestnut:
  - **Liechtenstein** — between [[Capitals-not-largest-city|Switzerland]] and Austria.
  - **Uzbekistan** — ringed by five landlocked neighbours (Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Afghanistan, Turkmenistan).
- **Only two landlocked countries in South America:** **Bolivia** and **Paraguay**.
- **Nepal** and **Bhutan** are landlocked between the giants **India** and **China**.

> [!tip] Hook
> "**Doubly landlocked**" almost always wants **Liechtenstein or Uzbekistan** — the only two. That's the single most-asked landlocked fact.

## Cluster
- **Africa** has the most landlocked countries of any continent (16).
- **Kazakhstan** is the **largest landlocked country** by area; **Ethiopia** the most populous.
- **Bolivia** lost its coast to Chile in the **War of the Pacific (1879–84)** and still maintains a navy on **Lake Titicaca**.
- The **Vatican City** and **San Marino** are landlocked microstates entirely within Italy.

## Mnemonic
Doubly landlocked = **"LU"** → **L**iechtenstein + **U**zbekistan. (The only two — nothing else.)

# Comic strips & superhero chestnuts

- ***Blondie*** — the platinum-blonde title character is the wife of **Dagwood Bumstead** (the strip that gave us the "Dagwood sandwich").
- ***Krazy Kat*** — early 20th-century strip; police dog **Officer/"Offissa" Pupp** protects Krazy Kat from the brick-throwing mouse Ignatz.
- ***Garfield*** creator **Jim Davis**; ***Dilbert*** creator **Scott Adams** (holds an MBA from Berkeley) — a recurring "strip creator's background" clue type.
- **The Green Hornet's** chauffeur/crime-fighting partner is **Kato** (see also [[Old-Time-Radio]]).
- ***Asterix*** — the diminutive Gaulish hero is aided by his oversized, menhir-carrying friend **Obelix** as they resist Roman occupation.

> [!tip] Hook
> Sidekick-to-hero recall is the dominant clue shape here: **Kato → Green Hornet, Obelix → Asterix, Offissa Pupp → Krazy Kat.**

## Cluster
- **Bucky Barnes** fought alongside **Captain America** in WWII-era Marvel continuity, later becoming the Winter Soldier.
- Marvel vs DC pairing chestnut: **Daredevil (Marvel) vs Batman (DC)** — both "ordinary human, no superpowers, driven by tragedy" heroes, frequently compared.
- **Deadpool (Marvel) vs Green Lantern (DC)** and **Storm (Marvel) vs Catwoman (DC)** are similarly paired in "which universe" quiz formats.
- **Thanos** (Marvel) is a cosmic supervillain; **Jonah Hex** (DC) is a Western antihero — an easy universe-identification pair once you know one anchor character per side.

## Mnemonic
Universe split: **Marvel = Daredevil, Deadpool, Storm, Thanos, Bucky. DC = Batman, Green Lantern, Catwoman, Jonah Hex.**

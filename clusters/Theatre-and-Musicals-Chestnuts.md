- ***Grease*** — central trio **Sandy, Danny Zuko, Rizzo**; set around **Rydell High**.
- ***Dreamgirls*** — modelled loosely on the Supremes; leads **Deena Jones, Effie White, James "Thunder" Early**.
- ***The King and I*** opens with **Anna's arrival in Bangkok** to tutor the King of Siam's children.
- ***Hairspray*** is set in **1960s Baltimore**.

> [!tip] Hook
> "Name that musical from its opening number" is the single most reliable clue shape in this sub-area — the **first big song** is usually more identifiable than the plot.

## Cluster
- ***Cats*** is based on T.S. Eliot's poetry collection *Old Possum's Book of Practical Cats*.
- ***Fiddler on the Roof*** is set in the (fictional) Russian shtetl of **Anatevka**.
- ***A Chorus Line*** is famous for its unusual structure: an entire show built around a single audition.
- Broadway vs West End: a musical's "**original cast recording**" is the standard reference point quizzes draw song lyrics from.

## Mnemonic
Opening-number recall: **Chorus Line = auditioning ("I Hope I Get It"), Fiddler = tradition, My Fair Lady = accent/English.**

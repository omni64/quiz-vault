> [!tip] Hook
> "This platform was renamed ___" is now a standing WQC current-affairs-adjacent clue type — **Twitter → X (2023)** is the highest-profile recent example, alongside **Facebook's parent company → Meta (2021)**.

## Cluster
- **Elon Musk** acquired Twitter in **October 2022** and rebranded it **X** in **July 2023**.
- Platform handle format ("@username") is itself sometimes the clue vehicle — matching a celebrity to their handle tests the same recall as a real-name chestnut (see [[Celebrity-Real-Names]]).
- **YouTube** (2005, founded by Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, Jawed Karim) was acquired by **Google** in 2006.
- Distinguish **founding date** (when the company/product launched) from **public launch date** (when it opened beyond invite-only beta) — quiz answers usually want the founding year unless specified.

# Television — character-to-show chestnuts

- ***The Office*** (US) — **Michael Scott, Dwight Schrute, Pam Beesly, Jim Halpert** — set at the fictional **Dunder Mifflin** paper company, Scranton, PA.
- ***Frasier*** — psychiatrist **Frasier Crane** moves back to Seattle to live with his father **Martin Crane**, a retired cop.
- ***Magnum, P.I.*** — Vietnam-vet private investigator **Thomas Magnum**, based in Hawaii, driving a red Ferrari.
- ***Boy Meets World*** — **Cory** and **Topanga** are the central will-they-won't-they couple.
- ***Ted Lasso*** — American football coach hired to manage fictional English football club **AFC Richmond**; journalist character **Trent Crimm**.

> [!tip] Hook
> "Character X is from which show" is the dominant TV clue shape — own the **lead ensemble names**, not just the show title.

## Cluster
- ***Growing Pains*** — patriarch is child psychiatrist **Jason Seaver**.
- ***Cheers*** spun off ***Frasier*** — a rare case of a sitcom spin-off outliving and out-awarding its parent (Frasier holds the record for most consecutive Emmy wins for a comedy series at the time).
- The "**mockumentary**" format — fake documentary crew, talking-head interviews — defines both *The Office* and *Parks and Recreation*.
- ***Hairspray*** (not a TV show but frequently confused with one) is set in **1960s Baltimore**.

## Mnemonic
Sitcom families: **Seaver** (Growing Pains), **Crane** (Frasier), **Scott** (The Office) — surname-first recall beats show-title-first recall for character ID clues.

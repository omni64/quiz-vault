# Current affairs — the last 12–18 months

Unlike every other cluster in this vault, this one has a **short shelf life by design**. WQC "current affairs" draws from roughly the year before the sitting, so this note needs a full refresh every few months, not just a fact-check. Everything below is dated; treat anything undated as unverified.

- **Pope Francis died 21 April 2025.** His successor, **Cardinal Robert Francis Prevost** (Chicago-born), was elected **8 May 2025**, taking the name **Pope Leo XIV** — the **first American-born pope** in history.
- **2025 Nobel Peace Prize:** **María Corina Machado**, Venezuelan opposition leader, for her pro-democracy campaign.
- **2025 Nobel Prize in Literature:** Hungarian author **László Krasznahorkai**.
- **2025 Nobel Prizes in the sciences:** Physics — **John Clarke, Michel Devoret, John Martinis** (macroscopic quantum tunnelling); Chemistry — **Susumu Kitagawa, Richard Robson, Omar Yaghi** (metal-organic frameworks); Medicine — **Mary Brunkow, Fred Ramsdell, Shimon Sakaguchi** (peripheral immune tolerance).
- **Notable deaths, 2025:** primatologist **Jane Goodall**; DNA co-discoverer **James Watson**; architect **Frank Gehry**; former US VP **Dick Cheney**; fashion designer **Giorgio Armani**.

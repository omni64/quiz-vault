# 58. Cerium (Ce)

**Discovered by:** Jöns Jacob Berzelius & Wilhelm Hisinger, 1803, Sweden; independently Martin Klaproth, 1803, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the dwarf planet Ceres (discovered 1801)
- Most abundant of all rare-earth elements
- "Mischmetal" (Ce-Fe alloy) is pyrophoric — used in lighter flints

## Common compounds
- CeO2 — glass-polishing compound, self-cleaning oven catalyst
- Mischmetal (lighter flints)

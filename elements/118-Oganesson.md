# 118. Oganesson (Og)

**Discovered by:** JINR Dubna, Russia with Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, USA — first synthesized 2002, named 2015-2016

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after physicist Yuri Oganessian — only the second element ever named after a living person (after seaborgium)
- Heaviest confirmed element on the periodic table; completes period 7
- Theoretical models suggest strong relativistic effects may make it behave atypically for a noble gas

## Common compounds
- None characterized — purely synthetic, too short-lived

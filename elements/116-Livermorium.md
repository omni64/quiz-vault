# 116. Livermorium (Lv)

**Discovered by:** JINR Dubna, Russia with Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, USA — first synthesized 2000, named 2011-2012

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, California
- Made by bombarding curium-248 with calcium-48 ions
- Predicted to be a heavy, volatile "metal" akin to polonium, but no chemistry experimentally confirmed

## Common compounds
- None characterized — too short-lived

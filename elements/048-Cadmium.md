# 48. Cadmium (Cd)

**Discovered by:** Friedrich Stromeyer, 1817, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named from "cadmia," an old term for zinc ore
- Highly toxic (linked to Itai-itai disease in Japan)
- Used in NiCd batteries and vivid yellow pigments

## Common compounds
- CdS — cadmium yellow pigment
- CdTe — thin-film solar cell material

# 89. Actinium (Ac)

**Discovered by:** André-Louis Debierne, 1899, Paris, France (independently also Friedrich Oskar Giesel, c. 1902, Germany)

## Unique properties / facts
- Glows pale blue in the dark from intense radioactivity ionizing surrounding air
- Lends its name to the entire "actinide" series
- Only trace amounts occur naturally in uranium ores

## Common compounds
- Ac2O3
- AcCl3 — studied only in trace quantities

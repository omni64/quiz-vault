# 31. Gallium (Ga)

**Discovered by:** Paul-Émile Lecoq de Boisbaudran, 1875, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Melts near body temperature (~29.8°C) — the "spoon that melts in hot tea" demo
- Confirmed Mendeleev's predicted "eka-aluminium"

## Common compounds
- GaAs — gallium arsenide (LEDs, semiconductors)
- Ga2O3

# 12. Magnesium (Mg)

**Discovered by:** Humphry Davy, 1808, England (isolated); recognized as distinct element by Joseph Black, 1755

## Unique properties / facts
- Burns with intensely bright white light (flares, fireworks)
- Lightest commonly-used structural metal
- Named after Magnesia, a district in Greece

## Common compounds
- MgSO4·7H2O — Epsom salt
- Mg(OH)2 — milk of magnesia
- MgO — magnesium oxide

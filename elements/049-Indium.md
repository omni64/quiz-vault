# 49. Indium (In)

**Discovered by:** Ferdinand Reich & Hieronymus Theodor Richter, 1863, Freiberg, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named for the indigo-blue line in its emission spectrum
- A very soft metal that emits an audible "cry" when bent
- Indium tin oxide (ITO) is the conductive coating in touchscreens/LCDs

## Common compounds
- ITO — indium tin oxide
- InP — indium phosphide semiconductor

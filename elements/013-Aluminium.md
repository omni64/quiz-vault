# 13. Aluminium (Al)

**Discovered by:** Hans Christian Ørsted, 1825, Denmark; refined by Friedrich Wöhler, 1827; industrial process 1886 (Hall, USA & Héroult, France, independently)

## Unique properties / facts
- Once pricier than gold before the Hall-Héroult process made it cheap
- Most abundant metal in Earth's crust

## Common compounds
- Al2O3 — alumina/corundum (ruby/sapphire base)
- KAl(SO4)2 — alum

# 83. Bismuth (Bi)

**Discovered by:** Known in medieval times; distinguished from lead/tin by Claude François Geoffroy, 1753, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Least toxic heavy metal — used in cosmetics and stomach medicine (Pepto-Bismol)
- Bi-209 has one of the longest known alpha-decay half-lives (~1.9×10^19 years), once thought stable
- Forms strikingly colorful iridescent crystals

## Common compounds
- Bismuth subsalicylate — Pepto-Bismol
- Bi2O3

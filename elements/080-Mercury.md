# 80. Mercury (Hg)

**Discovered by:** Known since antiquity (Egypt, China, India)

## Unique properties / facts
- Only metal liquid at room temperature
- Historically used in thermometers, barometers, and felt-hat manufacture ("mad as a hatter")
- Symbol Hg from Greek "hydrargyrum" (liquid silver)

## Common compounds
- HgCl2 — corrosive sublimate
- HgS — cinnabar
- Hg2Cl2 — calomel

# 34. Selenium (Se)

**Discovered by:** Jöns Jacob Berzelius, 1817, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Selene, Greek moon goddess (paired with tellurium, named for Earth)
- Essential trace nutrient but toxic in excess
- Photoconductive — used in old photocopiers/solar cells

## Common compounds
- Na2SeO3 — sodium selenite (supplement)
- H2Se

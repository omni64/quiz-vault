# 25. Manganese (Mn)

**Discovered by:** Recognized by Carl Wilhelm Scheele, 1774; isolated by Johan Gottlieb Gahn, 1774, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Used in dry-cell batteries and to decolorize glass
- Manganese steel alloys are exceptionally hard
- Essential trace nutrient

## Common compounds
- MnO2 — pyrolusite
- KMnO4 — potassium permanganate

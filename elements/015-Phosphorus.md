# 15. Phosphorus (P)

**Discovered by:** Hennig Brand, 1669, Germany (isolated from distilled urine)

## Unique properties / facts
- Glows in the dark (chemiluminescent — Greek for "light-bearer")
- White phosphorus is highly toxic and pyrophoric
- Essential to DNA, ATP, and bone

## Common compounds
- Ca3(PO4)2 — calcium phosphate (bone mineral)
- H3PO4 — phosphoric acid (in colas)

# 81. Thallium (Tl)

**Discovered by:** William Crookes, 1861, London, England (spectroscopically)

## Unique properties / facts
- Named from Greek "thallos" (green shoot) for its green spectral line
- Historically a favored poison ("inheritance powder") — tasteless and odorless
- Causes hair loss, a classic forensic diagnostic sign

## Common compounds
- Tl2SO4 — historical rodenticide
- TlBr/TlI — infrared optics

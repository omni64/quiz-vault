# 88. Radium (Ra)

**Discovered by:** Marie & Pierre Curie, 1898, Paris, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Glows faint blue-green in the dark; used in luminous watch dial paint, causing the "Radium Girls" scandal
- Roughly a million times more radioactive than an equal mass of uranium
- Name from Latin "radius" (ray)

## Common compounds
- RaCl2
- RaSO4 — historical luminous paint

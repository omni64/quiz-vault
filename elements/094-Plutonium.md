# 94. Plutonium (Pu)

**Discovered by:** Glenn Seaborg, Edwin McMillan, Joseph Kennedy, Arthur Wahl, 1940-41, Berkeley, California, USA

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Pluto (then classified as a planet)
- Core material of the "Fat Man" bomb dropped on Nagasaki
- Pu-238 powers RTGs on spacecraft including Voyager and Mars rovers

## Common compounds
- PuO2 — nuclear fuel/weapons
- PuF4

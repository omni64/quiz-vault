# 99. Einsteinium (Es)

**Discovered by:** Albert Ghiorso et al., 1952 (announced 1955), Berkeley/Argonne/Los Alamos, USA — found in debris of the "Ivy Mike" first thermonuclear test

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Albert Einstein
- Discovery kept classified for ~3 years since it came from H-bomb test debris
- So radioactive that its solutions visibly glow blue

## Common compounds
- Es2O3, EsCl3 — only ever produced/studied in nanogram quantities

# 66. Dysprosium (Dy)

**Discovered by:** Paul-Émile Lecoq de Boisbaudran, 1886, Paris, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Name from Greek "dysprositos" — "hard to get"
- Added to NdFeB magnets to improve heat resistance in EV motors
- One of the most magnetic elements known

## Common compounds
- Dy2O3
- Dy-doped NdFeB magnets

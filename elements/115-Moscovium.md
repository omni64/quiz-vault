# 115. Moscovium (Mc)

**Discovered by:** JINR Dubna, Russia with Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, USA — first synthesized 2003, named 2015-2016

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the Moscow region (Oblast), home of Dubna
- Made by bombarding americium-243 with calcium-48 ions
- Purely synthetic, only a handful of atoms ever produced

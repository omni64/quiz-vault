# 74. Tungsten (W)

**Discovered by:** Juan José & Fausto Elhuyar, 1783, Bergara, Spain

## Unique properties / facts
- Highest melting point of any metal (3422°C)
- Chemical symbol W from "wolfram"; used in incandescent light bulb filaments
- Tungsten carbide used for cutting tools/armor-piercing ammunition

## Common compounds
- WC — tungsten carbide
- WO3 — electrochromic windows

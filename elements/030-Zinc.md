# 30. Zinc (Zn)

**Discovered by:** Distilled in India/China from ~9th-14th century; scientifically isolated in Europe by Andreas Marggraf, 1746, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Brass (Cu+Zn) known since antiquity, long before pure zinc was isolated in Europe
- Used to galvanize steel against rust
- Essential trace nutrient

## Common compounds
- ZnO — zinc oxide (sunscreen/calamine)
- ZnS — sphalerite
- Brass (Cu-Zn alloy)

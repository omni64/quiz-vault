# 73. Tantalum (Ta)

**Discovered by:** Anders Gustaf Ekeberg, 1802, Uppsala, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Tantalus, for its chemical "tantalizing" inertness
- Extremely biocompatible/corrosion-resistant, used in surgical implants
- Tantalum capacitors are essential in smartphones

## Common compounds
- Ta2O5 — capacitors
- TaC — extremely hard carbide

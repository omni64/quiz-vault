# 71. Lutetium (Lu)

**Discovered by:** Georges Urbain, 1907, Paris, France (priority dispute with Carl Auer von Welsbach, Austria)

## Unique properties / facts
- Hardest and densest lanthanide
- LSO/LYSO lutetium crystals are the standard scintillator in PET scanners; Lu-177 used in cancer radiotherapy
- Named after Lutetia, the Roman name for Paris

## Common compounds
- LSO — lutetium oxyorthosilicate
- Lu2O3

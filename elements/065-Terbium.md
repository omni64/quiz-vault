# 65. Terbium (Tb)

**Discovered by:** Carl Gustaf Mosander, 1843, Stockholm, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- One of four elements named after the village Ytterby, Sweden
- Green phosphor in fluorescent lamps/old TVs
- Component of Terfenol-D, the alloy with the highest magnetostriction known

## Common compounds
- Tb2O3
- Terfenol-D (Tb-Dy-Fe alloy)

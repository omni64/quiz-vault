# 70. Ytterbium (Yb)

**Discovered by:** Jean Charles Galissard de Marignac, 1878, Geneva, Switzerland

## Unique properties / facts
- Fourth element named after Ytterby, Sweden
- Ytterbium optical lattice clocks are among the most precise atomic clocks ever built
- Used as a stress-gauge material in explosion/earthquake instrumentation

## Common compounds
- Yb2O3
- YbCl3

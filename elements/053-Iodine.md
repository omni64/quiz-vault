# 53. Iodine (I)

**Discovered by:** Bernard Courtois, 1811, France (from seaweed ash)

## Unique properties / facts
- Name from Greek "iodes" (violet) for its striking violet vapor
- Sublimes directly from solid to gas
- Essential for thyroid function (iodized salt)

## Common compounds
- KI — iodized salt/thyroid treatment
- AgI — cloud seeding & early photography
- Tincture of iodine (antiseptic)

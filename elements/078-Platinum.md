# 78. Platinum (Pt)

**Discovered by:** Known to pre-Columbian South American peoples; first scientific description by Antonio de Ulloa, 1748, Spain

## Unique properties / facts
- Used in catalytic converters to cut vehicle emissions
- Cisplatin, a platinum compound, is a major chemotherapy drug
- Extremely chemically inert ("noble" metal)

## Common compounds
- Cisplatin (Pt(NH3)2Cl2) — chemotherapy
- PtO2 — Adams' catalyst

# 35. Bromine (Br)

**Discovered by:** Antoine Balard, 1826, France

## Unique properties / facts
- One of only two elements liquid at room temperature (with mercury)
- Name from Greek "bromos" (stench)
- Widely used in flame retardants

## Common compounds
- AgBr — photographic film
- KBr — historic sedative

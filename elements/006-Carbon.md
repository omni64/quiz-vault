# 6. Carbon (C)

**Discovered by:** Known since antiquity (charcoal, soot)

## Unique properties / facts
- Only element with an entire field of chemistry named for it (organic chemistry)
- Diamond and graphite are both pure carbon
- Forms more known compounds than any other element

## Common compounds
- CO2 — carbon dioxide
- CaCO3 — limestone/chalk
- CH4 — methane

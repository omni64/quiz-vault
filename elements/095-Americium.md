# 95. Americium (Am)

**Discovered by:** Glenn Seaborg, Ralph James, Leon Morgan, Albert Ghiorso, 1944, Chicago/Berkeley, USA

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the Americas (parallels europium)
- Am-241 is the ionization source in nearly all household smoke detectors
- Its discovery was first announced publicly on a radio quiz show ("Quiz Kids," 1945) before formal publication

## Common compounds
- AmO2 — smoke detectors
- AmF3

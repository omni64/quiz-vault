# 16. Sulfur (S)

**Discovered by:** Known since antiquity ("brimstone")

## Unique properties / facts
- Associated with volcanic vents
- Key ingredient of gunpowder
- Rotten-egg smell actually comes from H2S, not sulfur itself

## Common compounds
- H2SO4 — sulfuric acid
- FeS2 — pyrite/"fool's gold"
- SO2 — sulfur dioxide

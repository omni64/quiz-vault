# 113. Nihonium (Nh)

**Discovered by:** Kosuke Morita's team, RIKEN, Japan — first synthesized 2004, officially credited by IUPAC December 2015, named 2016

## Unique properties / facts
- First element discovered and named by an Asian team — "Nihon" is a Japanese name for Japan
- Only a handful of atoms were detected in the confirmed discovery series
- Beat out a rival Dubna/Livermore claim that IUPAC did not credit

## Common compounds
- None characterized — purely synthetic, no observed chemistry

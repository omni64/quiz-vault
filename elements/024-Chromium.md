# 24. Chromium (Cr)

**Discovered by:** Louis Nicolas Vauquelin, 1797, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Name from Greek "chroma" (color)
- Cr3+ impurities make rubies red and emeralds green
- Chrome-plating gives shiny corrosion resistance

## Common compounds
- K2Cr2O7 — potassium dichromate
- PbCrO4 — chrome yellow pigment

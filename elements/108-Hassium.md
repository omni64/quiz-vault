# 108. Hassium (Hs)

**Discovered by:** Peter Armbruster, Gottfried Münzenberg et al., 1984, GSI Darmstadt, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Hesse (Hassia), the German state where GSI is located
- HsO4 synthesized/studied one atom at a time — found chemically similar to OsO4, confirming Hs as osmium's heavier homolog
- Predicted to be among the densest possible elements

## Common compounds
- HsO4 — hassium tetroxide, atom-by-atom, analog of osmium tetroxide

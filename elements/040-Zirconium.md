# 40. Zirconium (Zr)

**Discovered by:** Martin Klaproth, 1789, Germany (oxide); isolated by Jöns Jacob Berzelius, 1824, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the zircon gemstone
- Highly corrosion-resistant, used for nuclear reactor fuel cladding
- Cubic zirconia is a popular diamond simulant

## Common compounds
- ZrSiO4 — zircon
- ZrO2 — zirconia/cubic zirconia

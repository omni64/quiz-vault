# 8. Oxygen (O)

**Discovered by:** Carl Wilhelm Scheele (Sweden, ~1771-72) and Joseph Priestley (England, 1774) independently; named by Antoine Lavoisier, France

## Unique properties / facts
- ~21% of atmosphere
- Essential for combustion and respiration
- Ozone (O3) allotrope shields Earth from UV

## Common compounds
- H2O — water
- Fe2O3 — rust
- CO2 — carbon dioxide

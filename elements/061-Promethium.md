# 61. Promethium (Pm)

**Discovered by:** Jacob Marinsky, Lawrence Glendenin, Charles Coryell, 1945, Oak Ridge National Laboratory, USA

## Unique properties / facts
- Only lanthanide with zero stable isotopes — fully radioactive
- Named for Prometheus, symbolizing nuclear energy's danger/promise
- Used in luminous paint and betavoltaic "atomic batteries"

## Common compounds
- Pm2O3, PmCl3 — only ever handled in trace/research quantities

# 1. Hydrogen (H)

**Discovered by:** Henry Cavendish, 1766, England

## Unique properties / facts
- ~75% of all mass in the universe
- Only element with isotopes that have their own names (protium, deuterium, tritium)
- Powers stars via nuclear fusion

## Common compounds
- H2O — water
- NH3 — ammonia
- HCl — hydrochloric acid

# 86. Radon (Rn)

**Discovered by:** Friedrich Ernst Dorn, 1900, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Only radioactive noble gas; seeps from soil into basements — leading cause of lung cancer after smoking
- Briefly marketed in early 20th-century "radon spa" health treatments
- Densest gas known under normal conditions

## Common compounds
- RnF2 — only compound ever characterized, unstable

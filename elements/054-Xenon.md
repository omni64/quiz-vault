# 54. Xenon (Xe)

**Discovered by:** William Ramsay & Morris Travers, 1898, England

## Unique properties / facts
- Name means "stranger" in Greek
- Neil Bartlett's 1962 synthesis of XePtF6 was the first-ever noble gas compound
- Used in high-intensity arc lamps (film projectors)

## Common compounds
- XePtF6 — first noble-gas compound ever made (1962)
- XeF4

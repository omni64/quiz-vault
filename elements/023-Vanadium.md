# 23. Vanadium (V)

**Discovered by:** Andrés Manuel del Río, 1801, Mexico (retracted); independently rediscovered by Nils Gabriel Sefström, 1830, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Del Río found it first but was talked out of it by a French chemist; rediscovered 30 years later
- Named after Norse goddess Vanadis
- Strengthens steel alloys

## Common compounds
- V2O5 — vanadium pentoxide (sulfuric acid catalyst)
- FeV — ferrovanadium

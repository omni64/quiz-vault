# 90. Thorium (Th)

**Discovered by:** Jöns Jacob Berzelius, 1828, Stockholm, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Thor, Norse god of thunder
- Historically used in gas lantern mantles for its bright white glow
- Proposed as a potentially safer alternative nuclear fuel cycle to uranium

## Common compounds
- ThO2 — highest melting point of any oxide; gas mantles
- ThF4 — optical coatings

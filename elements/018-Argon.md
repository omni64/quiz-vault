# 18. Argon (Ar)

**Discovered by:** Lord Rayleigh & William Ramsay, 1894, England

## Unique properties / facts
- Name means "lazy/inactive" in Greek
- Most abundant noble gas in Earth's atmosphere (~1%)
- Fills incandescent bulbs and welding shielding gas

## Common compounds
- Forms virtually no stable compounds

# 38. Strontium (Sr)

**Discovered by:** Identified by Adair Crawford, 1790, Scotland; isolated by Humphry Davy, 1808, England

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Strontian, a village in Scotland
- Strontium salts give fireworks their red color
- Sr-90 is a notorious radioactive fallout isotope

## Common compounds
- SrCO3 — strontianite
- SrCl2 — sensitive-teeth toothpaste additive

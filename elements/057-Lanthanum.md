# 57. Lanthanum (La)

**Discovered by:** Carl Gustaf Mosander, 1839, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Name from Greek "lanthanein" (to lie hidden)
- First lanthanide discovered, lending its name to the whole series
- Used in high-refractive-index camera lens glass and NiMH battery electrodes

## Common compounds
- La2O3
- LaNi5 — hydrogen-storage alloy (NiMH batteries)

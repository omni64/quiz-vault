# 59. Praseodymium (Pr)

**Discovered by:** Carl Auer von Welsbach, 1885, Austria (separated from "didymium")

## Unique properties / facts
- Name from Greek "prasios didymos" ("green twin")
- Didymium glass (Pr+Nd) used in welder's/glassblower's goggles
- Alloyed with magnesium for high-strength aircraft engine parts

## Common compounds
- Pr6O11 — praseodymium oxide
- Didymium glass (Pr-Nd alloy)

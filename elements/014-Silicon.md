# 14. Silicon (Si)

**Discovered by:** Jöns Jacob Berzelius, 1824, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Second most abundant element in Earth's crust (after oxygen)
- Basis of modern semiconductor electronics ("Silicon Valley")
- A metalloid

## Common compounds
- SiO2 — quartz/sand
- SiC — carborundum (abrasive)
- Silicones (Si-O polymers)

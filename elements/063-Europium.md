# 63. Europium (Eu)

**Discovered by:** Eugène-Anatole Demarçay, 1901, Paris, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the continent of Europe
- Red/blue phosphor in old CRT/plasma screens and euro-banknote anti-counterfeiting
- Most chemically reactive rare-earth metal

## Common compounds
- Eu2O3 (phosphor)
- EuO (ferromagnetic semiconductor)

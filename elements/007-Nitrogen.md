**Discovered by:** Daniel Rutherford, 1772, Scotland

## Unique properties / facts
- ~78% of Earth's atmosphere
- Triple bond makes N2 extremely inert
- Liquid nitrogen used for cryopreservation

## Common compounds

- HNO3 — nitric acid
- N2O — laughing gas

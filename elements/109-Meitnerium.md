# 109. Meitnerium (Mt)

**Discovered by:** Peter Armbruster, Gottfried Münzenberg et al., 1982, GSI Darmstadt, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after physicist Lise Meitner, co-discoverer of nuclear fission — one of very few elements named after a woman
- Only a handful of atoms ever produced
- No experimental chemistry has ever been performed on it (too short-lived)

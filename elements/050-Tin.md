# 50. Tin (Sn)

**Discovered by:** Known since antiquity

## Unique properties / facts
- Alloyed with copper to make bronze (Bronze Age)
- "Tin cry" — a crackling sound when the metal is bent
- "Tin pest" — can disintegrate into grey powder in extreme cold

## Common compounds
- SnO2 — cassiterite (main tin ore)
- Bronze (Cu-Sn alloy)
- SnCl2

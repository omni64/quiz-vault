# 117. Tennessine (Ts)

**Discovered by:** JINR Dubna, Russia with Oak Ridge National Laboratory, Vanderbilt University, and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, USA — first synthesized 2010, named 2015-2016

## Unique properties / facts
- Named for Tennessee, honoring Oak Ridge (supplied the rare berkelium-249 target) and Vanderbilt
- One of the last two elements discovered to complete period 7 (with Og)
- Purely synthetic, only a handful of atoms ever produced

## Common compounds
- None characterized — too short-lived

# 19. Potassium (K)

**Discovered by:** Humphry Davy, 1807, England — first metal ever isolated by electrolysis

## Unique properties / facts
- Symbol K from Latin "kalium"
- Explosive reaction with water
- Essential body electrolyte

## Common compounds
- KNO3 — saltpeter (gunpowder)
- KCl — potash fertilizer
- KOH — caustic potash

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Ytterby, Sweden — the single village that lent its name to four elements (Y, Yb, Tb, Er)
- Used in red CRT-TV phosphors and YAG lasers

- YBa2Cu3O7 (YBCO) — famous high-temperature superconductor

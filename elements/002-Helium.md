# 2. Helium (He)

**Discovered by:** Pierre Janssen & Norman Lockyer, 1868 (solar spectrum); isolated on Earth by William Ramsay, 1895, Scotland

## Unique properties / facts
- First element discovered in space (sun) before being found on Earth
- Cannot be solidified at normal pressure
- Second-most abundant element in the universe

## Common compounds
- Forms virtually no stable compounds (noble gas)

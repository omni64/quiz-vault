# 42. Molybdenum (Mo)

**Discovered by:** Carl Wilhelm Scheele, 1778, Sweden; isolated by Peter Jacob Hjelm, 1781, Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Ore long confused with lead/graphite — name from Greek "molybdos" (lead)
- Strengthens steel alloys
- Essential to nitrogen-fixing bacteria enzymes

## Common compounds
- MoS2 — molybdenite (solid lubricant)
- MoO3

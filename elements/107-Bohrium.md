# 107. Bohrium (Bh)

**Discovered by:** Peter Armbruster, Gottfried Münzenberg et al., 1981, GSI Darmstadt, Germany

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after physicist Niels Bohr
- A handful of atoms have shown volatile oxychloride behavior consistent with group-7 (Re/Tc) chemistry
- All known isotopes have half-lives of seconds to a few minutes

## Common compounds
- BhO3Cl — bohrium oxychloride, single-atom gas chemistry only

# 9. Fluorine (F)

**Discovered by:** Henri Moissan, 1886, France

## Unique properties / facts
- Most reactive/electronegative element on the periodic table
- Several 19th-century researchers were poisoned or killed trying to isolate it ("fluorine martyrs")
- Used in Teflon and fluoridated toothpaste

## Common compounds
- CaF2 — fluorite/fluorspar
- NaF — fluoride toothpaste additive
- PTFE — Teflon

# 98. Californium (Cf)

**Discovered by:** Glenn Seaborg, Albert Ghiorso, Stanley Thompson, Kenneth Street Jr., 1950, Berkeley, California, USA

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after the state/University of California
- Cf-252 is an intense neutron emitter — used to start nuclear reactors, neutron radiography, and cancer brachytherapy
- One of the most expensive materials on Earth

## Common compounds
- CfCl3, Cf2O3 — very limited characterization

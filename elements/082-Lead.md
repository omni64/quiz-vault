# 82. Lead (Pb)

**Discovered by:** Known since antiquity (c. 7000 BC)

## Unique properties / facts
- Used extensively by Romans for pipes/cosmetics
- Lead-acid batteries remain the automotive standard
- Symbol Pb from Latin "plumbum" (root of "plumbing")

## Common compounds
- PbSO4 — lead-acid battery
- PbCrO4 — chrome yellow pigment

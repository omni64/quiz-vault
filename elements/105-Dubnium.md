# 105. Dubnium (Db)

**Discovered by:** JINR Dubna, USSR/Russia (1968-70), given discovery priority in IUPAC's 1997 resolution over a competing Berkeley claim

## Unique properties / facts
- Named directly after the city of Dubna, home of JINR
- A central battleground of the "Transfermium Wars" naming dispute
- Single-atom chemistry suggests group-5 (Nb/Ta) type behavior, with some anomalies

## Common compounds
- DbCl5, DbBr5 — single-atom-scale gas chemistry only

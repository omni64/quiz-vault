# 67. Holmium (Ho)

**Discovered by:** Marc Delafontaine & Jacques-Louis Soret (spectroscopic) and Per Teodor Cleve (isolation), 1878, Switzerland/Sweden

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Stockholm (Latin "Holmia")
- Highest magnetic permeability of any element
- Ho:YAG lasers used in surgery

## Common compounds
- Ho2O3 — spectrophotometer calibration standard
- Ho:YAG (laser crystal)

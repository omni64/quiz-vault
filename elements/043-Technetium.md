# 43. Technetium (Tc)

**Discovered by:** Carlo Perrier & Emilio Segrè, 1937, Palermo, Italy

## Unique properties / facts
- First artificially produced element ever (name from Greek "technetos" = artificial)
- Has no stable isotopes
- Tc-99m is the single most widely used medical diagnostic radioisotope worldwide

## Common compounds
- NH4TcO4 — ammonium pertechnetate (medical radiopharmaceuticals)
- Tc2O7

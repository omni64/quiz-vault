# 103. Lawrencium (Lr)

**Discovered by:** Jointly credited — Lawrence Berkeley Laboratory, USA (decisive 1971 work) and JINR Dubna, USSR (contributing 1965-70 work)

## Unique properties / facts
- Named after Ernest O. Lawrence, inventor of the cyclotron
- Final member of the actinide series
- Longest-lived isotope (Lr-266) has a half-life of only ~11 hours

## Common compounds
- None characterized — too short-lived
